Welcome NeuroTitans project!

Welcome to our project, "CIFAR-10 Image Classifier"! This application utilizes a convolutional neural network to accurately classify uploaded images into one of ten predefined categories from the CIFAR-10 dataset. Simply upload an image, and our advanced AI model will instantly identify and label it accordingly.
